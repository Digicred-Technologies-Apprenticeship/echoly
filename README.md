# Echoly

**Open-source GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) platform — track your brand's visibility across AI search engines.**

---

## What is Echoly?

People increasingly use AI platforms like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews to research, compare, and decide what to buy or use. When these tools generate an answer, they mention only a handful of brands and leave most others out. Businesses have no visibility into whether they're being mentioned, how they compare to competitors, or how to improve.

Echoly is a lightweight, self-hostable platform that helps brands measure and improve their presence in AI-generated answers — their **GEO score**.

## Who is it for?

- E-commerce & consumer brands
- B2B & SaaS companies
- High-consideration industries (finance, healthcare, legal, education)
- Content publishers & media
- Local & service businesses

## Core Features

- **Mention Tracking** — Run your prompts across major AI engines and track whether and how often your brand is mentioned.
- **Competitor Comparison** — Measure your Share of Voice against competitors.
- **Sentiment Analysis** — Understand whether mentions are positive, negative, or neutral.
- **AI Statement Capture** — See exactly what AI engines say about your brand.
- **Bot / Crawler Tracking** — Check whether AI crawlers (GPTBot, PerplexityBot, etc.) are visiting your site.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React
- **Backend:** Node.js + Express
- **Database:** MongoDB
- **AI Engines:** ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini (via API)
